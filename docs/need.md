### APPROACH: 
    Our approach was to gather and synthesize evidence from reddit threads which is 
    the most popular site to talk about something as casual and non-urgent as parks.

### CITATIONS: 
    WEBSITE 1: https://www.reddit.com/r/Seattle/comments/ozvtwl/park_recommendations/?onetap_auto=true&one_tap=true
    WEBSITE 2: https://www.reddit.com/r/Seattle/comments/11rnj3u/looking_for_places_to_visit_in_seattle_that_are/

### EVIDENCE:
    Website 1 shows evidence of a person looking for park recommendations in general. 
    Our app perfectly fulfills this as we take in the user's location and give them 
    nearby parks within their vicinity that can be filtered with our dropdown menu.

    Website 2 shows evidence of a person looking for places that are wheelchair accessible, 
    which is exactly what our app does. We are showing parks which, if need be, can be 
    filtered to have a wheelchair accessible entrance.
